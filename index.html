About us

Established in 2009, Flowers Across Melbourne aims to provide a quick, convenient service delivering flowers throughout Melbourne, including flowers to Melbourne CBD and outer Melbourne Suburbs.

Unlike many online florists who use relay services, all of our flowers are delivered direct from us. Flowers Across Melbourne is an online store which means we are not open to the public, allowing us to keep our prices affordable, however, we do offer a pickup option (please call to arrange pickup).

If you need flowers sent outside of Melbourne, Sydney and Brisbane metro areas, visit our online store Flowers Across Austalia to send flowers to anyone in VIC, NSW, QLD and SA directly via post!.
